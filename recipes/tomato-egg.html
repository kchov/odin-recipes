Tomato Egg

Description

If you're looking for a quick and nutricious meal, you've come to the right place. This Chinese egg dish is one of my personal favourites, and it can be made with simple ingredients that you would typically have in your pantry. Give it a go and let me know what you think of it!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and use a pair of chopsticks or a fork to beat the eggs.
  2. Chop the tomatoes into small chunks (2cm cubes).
  3. Heat up your pan to a medium-high temperature and add 2 tablespoons of butter, allow it to melt.
  4. Add the egg mixture into your pan, gently breaking the lumps into smaller pieces as it cooks. Dish out and set aside as soon as eggs are cooked.
  5. Clean the pan and heat it back up to medium high. Adding your remaining tablespoon of butter.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes into the pan and allow to cook until they are still slightly undercooked.
  7. Add your eggs back into the pan and cover it with the lid. Allow it to cook for a further 30 seconds. Dish out and serve immediately.
